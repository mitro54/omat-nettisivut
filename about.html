The biography

Well, that is me when i was a toddler.

I didn't start playing around with computers back then just yet,
other than ripping out all the damned keys of that keyboard in the picture...

I actually got my first computer when i was 8 years old, though it took me almost 24 years to figure out my love-hate relationship to these machines
and realize writing instructions to them is my passion.

Since the beginning i have figured most of these things on my own as i was raised by my grandparents, i was the only person understanding computers in the family.

But then when i was 16 i was faced with a tough question: "When i grow up, what do i want to be?" I would be lying if i told you that i had any clue. I thought maybe repairing cars could be fun because all of my closest friends went for that field, but i was pressured to take on the ICT-field because everyone thought that it was the most suited field for me. And it was. I just wasn't ready for it.

Long story short, i was in such a bad place in my teen years that most likely no matter what would have i done, would have failed. So i quit school on the last year. Skipping even longer story of my other misery and failures, i learned to be strong, independent, and most importantly, i learned to get shit done.

Here i am now stronger than ever. I started my Web Development journey end of 2023 and my goal has been to learn something new about computers every day. Going forward that will still be the plan.

I have been self-learning from Udemy and The Odin Project and i am also now in school where i seek to learn more about The Web Development.
